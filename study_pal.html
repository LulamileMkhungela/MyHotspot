StudyPal

Efficient way to study at home

Job Role:
User Research, UX Design and UI Design.

Duration:
3 Weeks

Tools Used:
AdobeXd,Paint3D,Paper&Pen

Platform:
Mobile App

Project Overview

Since the COVID-19 pandemic has isolated us all in our homes for a long period of time and the most affected group is little school going kids as this pandemic has put their future at stake. I thought of an app that fills up the gap between students and teachers as I couldn’t find any app that provides schooling experience digitally. This app is to help students stay organized with their different subject lessons/classes. Enable students to learn virtually at home and keep them connected to their classmates and teachers. Reminder for homework submission and calender to prepare individual students beforehand for upcoming quiz.

Problem

I saw my nephew struggling hard while attending class online via zoom call due to ongoing pandemic and teacher on other hand had to ask student multiple times to catch their attention. I understand people are managing it with zoom calls or skype etc, but they don’t provide students as well as teacher the proper class room experience. Though it does provide one-on-one interactions, But it has been noticed in alot of cases that students are caught doing idiotic things like howling, calling out names that annoys the teachers and disturbs other students who want to study. Additionally there is no proper recordings of videos just in case, if any student missed any call or couldn’t understand in one go due to internet issues. Having recordings can help teachers to plan their future lectures. There is no utility in zoom calls or skype calls where teachers can send assignments to students and students can submit them. Another thing that is hard for parents is keeping a constant check of their child's progress of assignments and quiz submissions. For little kids parents has to be around to monitor all the time unlike for middle school kids.

Solution

Overall theme including fonts, buttons, icons to be kept fun and colorful to keep students engaged. Live classes to join orelse have access to recorded video or written notes, if class is missed. Students to have saperate chat room to communicate with friends/classmates and teacher regarding lessons or extra curriculum activities. So if any student comes up with random questions during live class, teacher can direct them to ask privately in chatroom, it will minimize the unnecessary disturbance. Each student to have access to view due dates of quiz/assignment/homework in timely manner, no chance of forgetting or missing because notifications would be sent on the phone aswell. Calender in the app also has upcoming events highlighted Progress report with graph and average marks after every quiz result is updated for students, to push themselves for better grades and also provide convenience for parents, to stay updated about their child performance. Reimagined all needed features that could be designed all in one app. I started research, sketch out my ideas to design an app particularly for school going kids to provide them classroom experience digitally.

User Research & Data

#1

Before I began my design project, I conducted survey interviews with 16 parents of school-age kids to get a better understanding of whom I was designing this app for: Since this app is planned to be elementary and middle school kids. So among the 16 parents I interviewed, there were mostly young females parents. The age of the parents ranged from 24 to 38 years old, while couple of female parents over the age of 40 years old. Out of which 43.8% had elementary school-age children and 12.5% had middle school-age children.

#2

I felt like taking opinion on whether parents still prefer remote learning for their kids or not. And currently what digital solution they are using for it. Over 50% parents still are in favor of online schooling and 25% combintion of both remote and in school. Mostly are using zoom or google meet.

#3

To my surprise, honesty of these ladies while taking this survey needs some applause. When asked about current online learning situation, none of them find it bad but on the other hand not so great too, just on the satisfactory level. Additionally, 90% of the ladies find their kids struggle to keep constant interest and focus.

#4

To make this even more interesting, I asked about different challenges they face as a family due to current learning setup. Timing of parents and children to match for monitoring and learning can clearly be hard. Additionally, mostly parents do admit that they are unable to stay updated about their child performace/progress level. 33% children are unable to balance quiz and homework submission.
Now that you have a better understanding of why and who I was designing this app for, it has become easy to gather basic needs and functionality goals for this application.

Research insights

This app needs to enable the user to:

  1. Students to join class and access previous lecture recording or notes .
  2. Chat with teachers privately for any queries and chat with friends for discussion.
  3. Have their own calender for quiz and assignments due dates to be marked.
  4. Submit assignments/homework on timely manner.
  5. Be notified about their upcoming quiz.
  6. Have their updated progress report.
Clients Needs

This application needs to enable the client to:

  1. Emphasis on growth and development, so teachers are motivated to improve classroom learning conditions for their students.
  2. A shared platform for a one-stop solution for students, teachers and parents.
  3. A platform that helps in effective nationwide adoption in education sector.
  4. Progress Chart to make it easier for parents to monitor the development of their children.
    5. functionalities
    1. App will allow each student to register and have a personal account .
    2. Account enables student to stay connected with his/her instructor and other classmates/students .
    3. Different levels and subjects consist of lectures, upcoming quiz and related homework .
    4. Each student account has its own calender to view higlighted dates for assignments and upcoming quiz that enables students to prepare ahead of time.
    5. Account also allows student to view their progress report which motivates them with each passing level.
    6. Notifications are also actively sent to inform about upcoming events. .

FINAL SCREENS

Welcome Sceen

Once after downloading and opening the app for the first time. The welcome screen would appear that has the background of a chalkboard, I created using Adobe PHotoshop. Individual gets the option to register, if he/she is not a student already and login button if you are already a member. To register, every student has to add their name, email address, password, location, school and date of birth.

Based on the information collected, I adapted the chosen wireframe to design the final screens.

Profile

After Registering, student can select his/her grade and get access to their acount. The acount has their picture, main information and buton link to direct them towards their personal calender, subject classes, quiz and homework plus progress report.

MY CLASSES:

This button link from their profile will direct the student to select a subject according to his/her grade. Every specific subject has it details, time duration, textbook image that student has in hand. Button options to join session or access the recorded lessons. Next quiz and due homework for this subject.

QUIZ & PROGRESS REPORT:

This quiz button link from profile will direct the student to all the upcoming subject's quiz that they have to give with the information of time duration and number of questions they are given to finish each quiz.
Th button from the profile directs the student to their weekly progress bar-graph according to level of performance

HOMEWORK:

This homework button link from profile will direct the student to all the subject's homework that is due and once they complete it, a success mark will appear opposite to that subject in the list.

MY CALENDAR:

This calender button will direct the user to it personal calender. Yellow highlighted date indicates assignment/homework due date and red highlighted date indicates upcoming quiz.

Navigation Bar

Navigation bar at the bottom has five of my handmade icons. From left to right: Which includes Classes - Progress report - Calender - Friends - profile

Usability Testing

To make sure this app is designed truely to student needs so I stayed focused on the insights I was able to fetch from the research throughout the design process.

For this project I conducted usability testings - the Guerrilla testing between the wireframing sketch and high-fidelity and after prototyping, to check if my iteration worked.
The testings were extremely effective for identifying the minor issues in my prototype and to improve the student's experience while using the app.
Moreover, to let these issues to be fixed with much less time and lower cost, It's necessary that usability testing is conducted during design process.
I ran the usability testing by sharing the prototype with 5 young parents with their school-age kids. I asked them to complete major tasks, register/login, creating profile, analysing features of calender and progress report... etc. I observed them remotely online through screen that was shared with me.

Usability

Main Concerns Were:

  1. Students wouldn't know how to use the app for the first time.
  2. Progress report is only for weekly update, not month or yearly.
  3. No option of muting or leaving a chatroom.
  4. Teacher remarks on progress report.
  5. Option for sign in specifically for teachers, and manage their classroom.
Solutions
  1. Add an instruction section in the begining for guidance.
  2. Add an option to view progress graph on month/yearly report.
  3. Arrows to direct user to view next or previous month, not just the current month.
  4. Adding a setting option within each chatroom to mute or leave if user wills.
  5. Not considered, but not a bad idea for the app, if we add this feature in future.
  6. Definitely another version of app for teachers only to be designed and is already been considered.
