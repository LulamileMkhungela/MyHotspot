Travelo App

Designing a travel community app which will gain attraction to over million+ users

Overview

About the project

This is a two-years-history of how we designed Travelo app, a travel community app with estimation of +1,5 million members it will have,and allow users to exchange their skills for accommodation around the world.

Timeline: 5 weeks

Tools Used: AdobeXd,Adobe Photoshop,Paper&Pen

My Role: I was the Junior Visual Designer of the Travelo App until 2018. I collaborated with a Product Manager, a Product Marketing and the Development Team to research, scope, plan and execute.

The Challenge

To comply with my non-disclosure agreement, I have omitted confidential information in this case study. All information is my own and does not necessarily reflect the views of Travelo.
Travelo is a community based on collaboration that makes travel more accessible to those seeking a profound cultural experience. Through an online platform, we connect travelers – looking to exchange their skills for accommodation – with incredible hosts from all around the world.
We decided to launch an app to help travelers to find and confirm their Travelo experience on the go.
I was part of the project of building the Travelo app to give users a better experience whether chilling in a chair at home or heading to work in a crowded train.

First touch

Onboarding: An understanding about what Travelo is all about.

Travelo invests so much in acquiring new users, we definitely didn’t want to lose them after their very first visit. Besides that, it was important to create a personal experience onboarding those users and lead them to a brand connection & personalised content throughout the app usage.

That’s why we introduced an onboarding asking specific questions to build a path to relevant results, explaining what was the app for and create a way to let travers say their travel plans at a glance.
To define the onboarding questions, we interviewed the stakeholders and conducted interviews asking travelers to priositise the most important factors in deciding to book a trip with Travelo.

Search

Once travelers got the full understanding of what Travelo is all about, they need to find a the right position to apply and start their journeys.
Based user interviews, we inserted the most relevant filters to guide user to search based on their characteristics, what they were good at and their Inspirations in the trip

Hosts

Finding a perfect host means to find a place where you could match your purpose on the trip with the need of the supply side. To make it clear at a glance, we devided the host listing explanation in two parts.
What you offer: with big icons explaining the main tasks and requirements.
What to get: highlighting rewards and benefits by applying to that position.
The host listing was also decided in other factors that contribuit to the users choices such as availability, requirements, photos and reviews.
