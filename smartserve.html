SmartServe

Redefining restaurants ordering experience using SmartServe App.

Overview

About the project

In this project, we present a smartphone application which solves some of the major problems faced by grocery shoppers by integrating existing technologies and services to help people save time, money and create a memorable shopping experience.

Timeline: 2 Months

My Role: Interaction Design, Prototyping & Evaluation

The Challenge

How can we improve restaurants ordering system experience?

Although we have all the technology at our disposal, the ordering experience has not much advanced with the times. A lot of customers shoppers still face inconveniences in finding restaurants and order without the interference of waiter(ss), giving feedback on food been served to you & spending time in checkout lines and find out what you want to order is unavailable.
