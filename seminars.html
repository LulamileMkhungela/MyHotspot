Portfolio review and Redesign Your Portfolio - 90 mins
R370 per hour (1 person)

The goal of these sessions is to offer feedback that helps you prepare your online portfolio as a Designer or Developer for hiring manager review and to practice going through the interview process.

Using Microsoft Teams / BlueJeans video conferencing platforms, I will go through your portfolio with you, and help you to ensure it’s ready for submission. Then we will spend time on redesigning your portfolio together and expected interview questions to expect if you Looking for a job.
